SLAFES XXV: Advancing Together in Solid State Physics.

Welcome to the XXV Latin American Symposium on Solid State Physics (SLAFES) from October 23 to 26, 2024, at Universidad del Atlántico, Sede Norte, Puerto Colombia. A prominent event in Latin America for exchanging information and discussing innovations in Solid State Physics.

Join international experts at SLAFES 2024 to explore the latest advancements and contributions in this strategic area of knowledge.
